Champion Mastery Lookup

About this tool



I made a quick and simple website that lets you view how many mastery points other players have on more than just the 11 (3 top + 8 in progress) champions you can see in the League of Legends client. It doesn't have any special features, but if people like it and/or Riot takes a while implementing a similar feature in the client, I may add more stuff
